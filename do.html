What We Do.

We supply and apply a complete range of paints, coatings and lacquers to your specifications. We can Help you in the selection of appropriate coatings to suit your particular requirements. We can add the finishing touches to your producy by the application of silk-screened images in epoxy or acrylic inks as required.

Extensive range of Paints:

-Polyurethane(two-pack)
-Acrylic and Epoxys
-Stoving enamels
-Waterbone coatings(Solid and Metallics)

Our Range of Finishes and Pre-tratments:

-Anodising (range of colours)
-Hard Anodising
-Gold Iriditing
-Clear Chromating

We can provide On-site spraying of metals, plastics and woods can be carried out where required. We carry out precision spraying for some of the best-known multinational companies involved in the Healthcare, Computer, Electronics, and other high-tech industries. In summary, We are a solution provider when it comes to choosing the supplier for all your finishing needs.
