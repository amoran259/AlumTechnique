About Us.

AlumTechnique has been operating the last 25 years. It started of as a one man company which now we have up to 5 employees.The aim of Alumtechnique is to continue providing the highest quality spray painting and coating. We provide a wide range of expertise across our wide ranges of paints and techniques. Some examples of recent concracts undertaken is decorative lighting, Architectural features, Cosmestic refurbishment of a variety of household items including wooden presses and tables. We also have experience in spraying electical housing equipments such as Pc's and laptops and provide application of black optical finishes for the medical industry.
